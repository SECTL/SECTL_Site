Server Rules

These rules may change at any time. The server owner's latest published version shall prevail.

Cheating

  1. Explicitly allowed assisting features: X-Ray, ESP, entity highlighting, full brightness, freecam, auto-click (not allowed for PvP), auto-fishing, projection, printer, and note block music performance. Any other cheating features are not allowed.
  2. If you knowingly benefit from another player using disallowed cheats, it will be treated as using disallowed cheats.

Buildings & Machines

  1. It is recommended to label ownership for any building, machine, or container storing your items using a sign in a visible place to avoid disputes. You may use Bolt or WorldGuard to protect containers, buildings, and machines from others.
  2. Before modifying or dismantling someone's building, taking their items, or starting or stopping their machines, you must get owner's permission. Naturally generated structures are not restricted.

How do we determine ownership?

  • Prefer ownership signs, Bolt, or WorldGuard data
  • If none exists, admins may use CoreProtect. The earliest placer within last 7 days (of it or part of it) is considered owner
  • If ownership still cannot be determined, it is treated as ownerless and may be handled freely
  1. Always turn off machines after use.
  2. Do not build machines that cause performance issues (MSPT > 60 or TPS < 10). "Tech" that uses more than 3 fake players is also not allowed.

Examples (including but not limited to):

  • Binary-tree 2048 furnace array: https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1bA7Kz4EYV
  • Grass-sheep machine matrix larger than 3x3: https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1YN83zCEb3
  • Idle bare-handed fake-player area: https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1cg8yzJEAn
  • No-player tower with furnace array: https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV17YWPzSEAB
  1. Do not build anything that causes client lag for others (FPS drops by more than half of their original FPS).

If ownership can be determined, team will try to negotiate with you and vote before dismantling; it won't be done lightly.

If ownership cannot be determined, team may dismantle it directly after a successful admin vote.

Chat & Language

Note: These rules apply to both in-game chat and the QQ group chat.

  1. No illegal speech (including but not limited to pornography, gambling, drugs, political provocation, etc.). Punishments for this rule cannot be appealed.
  2. No personal attacks, discrimination, or flame wars.
  3. If more than two people engage in a serious argument in chat, all participants will be punished regardless of who is "right".
  4. No content that is likely to disgust or offend others.

Player Interaction

  1. PvP is only allowed with explicit consent from the other player.
  2. If your legitimate rights are harmed, report to admins instead of retaliating. We will not reduce punishment for violations committed during retaliation.

Admin Code

We assume you have learned the basics from existing admins. In summary:

  • #1) Respect others' privacy.
  • #2) Consider consequences and risks before executing any command.
  • #3) With great power comes great responsibility.
  1. Admins should serve players with dedication, maintain server order, and protect player rights.
  2. Admins must not abuse their powers. Abuse includes:
  • Covering up or assisting rule-breaking
  • Extortion using admin status
  • Issuing punishments or dismantling or modifying builds without a vote
  • Personal attacks, discrimination, or malicious mockery against players
  1. All decisions require a vote by all admins to ensure fairness and transparency.
  2. Bans, mutes, early unbans, appeal approval, or small rollbacks require at least 1/2 of admins.
  3. Dismantling or modifying others' builds or facilities, building large projects, or large rollbacks require at least 2/3 of admins.
  4. Rollbacks and other very large operations require at least 2/3 of admins and must be approved or executed by the owner.
  5. The owner always has a final approve or veto vote on admin decisions.

Punishments

If you violate any rule above, you may receive one or more of the following penalties:

  • Warning and request to stop the violation (remove illegal machines / uninstall illegal plugins / disable illegal features, etc.)
  • Temporary or permanent removal of Creative / Admin permissions (temporary removal keeps you in the admin team but you cannot use admin powers anywhere; permanent removal means removal from the admin team and you may not re-apply)
  • Ban for 1 day / 3 days / 7 days / 1 month / 1 year
  • Permanent ban
