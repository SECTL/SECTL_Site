本规则适用于 SECTL 组织下所有项目及相关社区。

禁止行为

在社区交流时，请遵守相关法律法规，严禁发布以下内容：

  1. 违法违规：涉黄、涉赌、涉毒、恐怖暴力、涉政等敏感或违法言论。
  2. 恶意内容：病毒、木马、外挂等恶意软件或链接。
  3. 广告推销：未经许可的商业广告或推广信息。
  4. 干扰秩序：刷屏、恶意复读、频繁骚扰他人等干扰正常交流的行为。
  5. 不当言论：人身攻击、辱骂、引战等破坏社区氛围的言论。

提问与求助

为了更高效地解决您的问题，提问前请遵循以下建议：

  1. 先尝试自查：查阅官方文档、常见问题或使用搜索引擎。
  2. 提供详细信息：
    • 环境信息：操作系统版本、软件版本。
    • 问题描述：发生了什么、如何复现（步骤）。
    • 诊断信息：提供错误截图、录屏或程序日志。
  3. 端正态度：提问时请保持礼貌，遵循 《提问的智慧》。

❌ 错误示范："软件打不开怎么办？"（无任何信息）

✅ 正确示范："我在 Windows 11 上使用 SecRandom v2.2.0，点击启动后无反应，这是我的错误日志截图..."

违规处理

违反上述规定者，管理员将视情节轻重采取以下措施：

  • 口头警告 / 撤回违规内容
  • 禁言处理（24小时 - 30天）
  • 永久移除出群 / 拉黑

希望大家共同维护一个友好、互助的社区环境！

These rules apply to all projects and related communities under the SECTL organization.

Prohibited Behavior

When communicating in the community, please comply with relevant laws and regulations. Strictly prohibit posting the following content:

  1. Illegal or Regulated Content: Pornography, gambling, drugs, terrorism, violence, politics, or other sensitive or illegal statements.
  2. Malicious Content: Viruses, trojans, cheats, malware, or malicious links.
  3. Advertising and Promotion: Unauthorized commercial advertisements or promotional information.
  4. Disruptive Behavior: Spamming, malicious repetition, frequent harassment, or other behavior that disrupts normal communication.
  5. Inappropriate Language: Personal attacks, insults, flame wars, or other behavior that damages community atmosphere.

Questions and Support

To more efficiently solve your problems, please follow these suggestions before asking:

  1. Self-check First: Consult official documentation, FAQ, or use search engines.
  2. Provide Detailed Information:
    • Environment Info: Operating system version, software version.
    • Problem Description: What happened and how to reproduce it (steps).
    • Diagnostic Info: Provide error screenshots, screen recordings, or program logs.
  3. Maintain Proper Attitude: Please be polite when asking questions and follow How To Ask Questions The Smart Way.

❌ Bad Example: "What should I do if the software won't open?" (no information)

✅ Good Example: "I'm using SecRandom v2.2.0 on Windows 11. The app won't respond when I click launch. Here's my error log screenshot..."

Violation Handling

Those who violate the above rules will be subject to the following measures by administrators based on the severity:

  • Verbal warning / Removal of violating content
  • Mute (24 hours - 30 days)
  • Permanent removal from group / Blacklist

We hope everyone will work together to maintain a friendly and supportive community environment!
